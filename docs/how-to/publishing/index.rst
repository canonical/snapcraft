.. _how-to-publishing:

Publishing
==========

Once a snap is built and tested, it's ready for publication to the Snap Store or a
private store. These guides cover the different tasks you will take

Before you can publish your snap, you need to log in with the destination store.

- :ref:`how-to-authenticate`

For all stores, a snap must be registered before it can be made public and built
remotely. Once registered, snaps are published as atomic revisions with progressive
levels of stability.

- :ref:`how-to-register-a-snap`
- :ref:`how-to-publish-a-snap`

After you publish a snap, its revisions and channels must be maintained and monitored.
You can also obtain usage metrics about it.

- :ref:`how-to-check-a-snaps-status`
- :ref:`how-to-manage-revisions-and-releases`
- :ref:`how-to-get-snap-metrics`
